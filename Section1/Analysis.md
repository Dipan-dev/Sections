# Analysis Before Designing

## Section 1 — Community CTA Section

### Layout & Content
It is a **full-width centered section** with a clean, minimal layout. It contains:
- A bold `h1` heading — **"Visit our Community"**
- A short supporting paragraph beneath it
- A **CTA button** — "Go to Community" with a bright green (`#22C55E` approx) fill and dark text, using a large `border-radius` (pill shape)

### Font & Typography
- Font family appears to be **"Inter"** or similar sans-serif
- The `h1` is very large, heavy weight (`font-weight: 800` or `900`), and white colored
- The paragraph is lighter weight, muted white/gray, and centered below the heading

### Color & Background
- Background is **pure black** (`#000000`) — stark and minimal, no gradients
- This contrasts heavily with the **vibrant green button**
- The green acts as the **only accent color** in the entire section, drawing all attention to the CTA

### Design Challenges
- **Contrast management** — pure black + white text is high contrast; keeping it accessible
  without feeling harsh is key
- **Button sizing** — the pill button is unusually wide, almost like a full-width element on
  smaller viewports; replicating that proportional feel responsively is tricky
- **Whitespace balance** — the section relies entirely on generous padding/margin to feel
  premium; too little space and it collapses visually
