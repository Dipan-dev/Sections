# SoluLab AI Chatbot — Frontend Development Analysis

A detailed breakdown of learnings, decisions, and growth across the full development lifecycle of the SoluLab AI Chatbot landing page — from initial planning to final implementation.

## Before Development

- Initial Understanding of the Design
- Before writing a single line of code, the design was analyzed section by section from the Figma file. Here is what I understood at the start:

- The page had a dark navy theme **(#0a1628 background)** for the hero and feature sections
- The layout was two-column **'flexbox'** — text on the left, illustration on the right
- The right side contained a 3D isometric robot illustration with floating shapes
- There was a "Why Choose SoluLab" section with a 2×2 card grid on a black background
- A white background section with a dark image box on the left and text on the right

- At this stage the design looked straightforward — just layout and colors. The complexity was not yet visible.
